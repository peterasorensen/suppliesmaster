DairyAmerica

(Processor - COOP)

Back to Search Results New Search

DairyAmerica, Inc. is a federated marketing organization comprised of 4 dairy cooperative owners; Agri-Mark Inc., California Dairies Inc., O-AT-KA Milk Products Inc., and United Dairymen of Arizona. DairyAmerica delivers nutrition to more than 60 countries each year, providing full logistic and documentation support to our partners. With annual exports of more than 200,000 metric tons for the past several years, the DairyAmerica brand has become one of the most recognized brands worldwide. Our products are found in cheese, yogurt, confectionary, bakery, UHT, condensed/sweetened condensed milk, infant formula, and other product applications across the globe.

Since 1921, First District Association has been a grassroots dairy cooperative located in Litchfield Minnesota. Their new state of the art cheese plant, whey protein dryer and lactose dryer, allows them to process 7.5 million pounds of milk from our family farms per day. Their manufacturing facilities are SQF certified as well as USDA and FDA approved. In addition, all three facilities comply with the requirements of the Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI) for food safety systems allowing them to ensure consistency, traceability and responsiveness at every step of their manufacturing processes yielding high quality products and ingredients. Their cheese, whey products and lactose are Kosher, Halal and EU approved. In addition, they have a state of the art laboratory on premises with the equipment needed to test for pathogens as well as the technical expertise that allows them to meet their customers’ specifications. They are also proud to say that 100 percent of our dairy farmers actively participate in the National Dairy Farm Animal Care Program. High Desert Milk

(Processor - COOP)

Back to Search Results New Search

High Desert Milk was formed in 2001 by six Idaho dairymen who wanted to vertically integrate their operations. In 2008 the company began producing Nonfat Dry Milk from their Burley, Idaho facility. In 2012 an expansion was built to include manufacture of butter and buttermilk powder. The focus at High Desert Milk is quality and this means being in control of every facet of dairy operations from the field to the fork. A majority of all the feeds used is raised on our own farms. Milking operations are monitored for quality by our own, in-house, state-certified lab facility. Milk is hauled by High Desert’s fleet of tankers daily, and delivered directly to the plant for processing. The haul is local, since all dairies are located less than 30 miles from the facility. All milk is received and processed the day that it is delivered, allowing us to produce a quality low spore, heat-stable product that can be used in a variety of applications including infant formula.
