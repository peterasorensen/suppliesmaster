International Ingredient Corporation (IIC) is a family owned manufacturer and supplier of specialty ingredients to the animal feed and pet food industries. IIC has a total of ten plant locations in the US, including drying plants and dry blending facilities. Feed-grade dairy products are a significant proportion of our product line and include Dairylac® 80 (roller dried whey permeate), Spray Dried Dairylac® 80, Nutri-Gold® Dried Milk, Dried Cheese products, Cheese-Whey, Chocolate dairy products, lactose, and milk proteins. We are the experts in supplying dairy ingredients to the animal feed and pet food industries.
